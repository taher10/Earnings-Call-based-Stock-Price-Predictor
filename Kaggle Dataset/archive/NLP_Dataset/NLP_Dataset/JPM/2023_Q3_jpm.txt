Similarly, Asset & Wealth Management reported a flat revenue at $4.6 billion, balanced by higher management fees and inflows against lower performance fees, underlining the firm's ability to pivot and capitalize on market opportunities for growth.Corporate Sector and 2023 ProjectionsThe corporate segment reported a sharp rise in net income to $911 million, with revenue surging by $1.8 billion compared to last year, driven primarily by increases in net interest income as a consequence of higher rates.
